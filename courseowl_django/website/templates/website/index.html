About Us

With the abundance of possible classes that one can take online, it is highly unlikely a student will ever get to see or take some of the ones that are most interesting to them. We aim to overcome this obstacle by developing a system that will provide recommendations from massive open online courses to people based on their interests and courses they have taken.
