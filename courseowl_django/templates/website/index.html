About Us

With the abundance of possible classes that one can take online, it is highly unlikely that a student be able to discover interesting and relevant courses, especially across multiple providers. We aim to overcome this obstacle by developing a system that will provide recommendations from massive open online courses to people based on their interests and courses they have taken.
